<h3 align="center">About Me</h3>

<p>I am a Master's student in Electronic and Computer Engineering, combining a strong academic foundation with hands-on experience in hardware and software integration. My passion lies in transforming complex technological challenges into functional, reliable solutions, particularly within the fields of Computer Vision, Embedded Systems, Robotics, and Automation.</p>

<p>Starting with a background in programming, I intentionally transitioned into hands-on electronics roles to bridge the gap between software and hardware. This practical exposure inspired me to formally study the field and graduate. Today, my goal is to take on innovative projects that challenge my abilities and help me specialize in my true technical passion.</p>

<p>Driven by this eagerness to learn, I am expanding my expertise in microcontroller architectures and Real-Time Operating Systems (RTOS). As I grow my professional network, I am always open to connecting with industry peers, sharing insights, and collaborating on impactful engineering projects.</p>
